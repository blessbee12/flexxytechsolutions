OUR STORY

Who We Are

Flexxy Tech Solutions is a Botswana-based ICT company committed to delivering affordable, high-quality technology services to businesses and students. Founded by Blessing Keabetswe Rankunyana, we began as a small team with one simple belief — every business, no matter how small, deserves exceptional digital solutions.

From our humble beginnings to now serving clients across Botswana, we've stayed true to our core values while continuously evolving with the ever-changing technology landscape.
