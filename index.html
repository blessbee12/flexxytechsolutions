About Flexxy Tech

Founded in 2024 by Blessing Keabetswe Rankunyana, Flexxy Tech Solutions is a Botswana-based ICT company focused on delivering affordable, high-quality technology services to businesses and students.

We combine technical expertise with business knowledge to create solutions that are practical, effective, and tailored to our clients' specific needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology partner in Botswana and beyond, known for empowering businesses through smart, accessible, and innovative digital solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver cost-effective and high-quality tech services tailored to client needs while contributing to the digital growth of businesses, students, and startups.
